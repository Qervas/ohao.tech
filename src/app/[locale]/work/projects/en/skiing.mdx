This cutting-edge research project focused on developing a real-time skiing movement classifier model, pushing the boundaries of sports analytics and computer vision:

- Achieved an impressive 80% accuracy in classifying complex skiing movements
- Utilized OpenPose for precise acquisition of human skeleton coordinates from video frames
- Implemented advanced STGCN (Spatial Temporal Graph Convolutional Network) to extract sophisticated features from input coordinates
- Published a groundbreaking academic paper with team members, contributing to the field of sports science and AI

This project demonstrates the powerful intersection of artificial intelligence and sports analysis, opening new avenues for athlete training and performance optimization.

## Technical Details

- **Framework:** STGCN (Spatial Temporal Graph Convolutional Network)
- **Vision Model:** OpenPose
- **Programming Language:** Python
- **Development Period:** 2021
